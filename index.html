Composting in NYC 2020-2025

Composting has risen sharply since 2024

By Chloe Rogers

Sunday, December 7

All five boroughs saw a significant increase in composting since 2020.

According to NYC Open Data:

  • The Bronx increased its residential composting by 505%
  • Brooklyn increased its residential composting by 884%
  • Manhattan increased its residential composting by 717%
  • Queens increased its residential composting by 661%
  • Staten Island increased its composting by 2523%.
